### Three Interactive Operations

#### 1. **Position (Drag)**
- **Action**: Click and drag anywhere inside the crop box
- **Effect**: Moves the image position
- **Range**: -50% to 150% (horizontal & vertical)
- **Visual**: Hand cursor, real-time movement

#### 2. **Crop (Corner Handles)**
- **Action**: Click and drag any of the 4 blue corner circles
- **Effect**: Resizes the visible crop area
- **Range**: 20% to 100% (width & height)
- **Visual**: Resize cursors, live crop preview

#### 3. **Scale (Mouse Wheel)**
- **Action**: Hold Shift + Scroll mouse wheel
- **Effect**: Zooms the image in/out
- **Range**: 50% to 200%
- **Visual**: Image scales smoothly

## Step-by-Step Usage

### Quick Start
1. **Enable Interactive Mode**
   - Click "🎯 Enable Interactive Mode" button
   - Visual overlay appears on poster

2. **Position the Image**
   - Click and drag inside the blue box
   - Image moves in real-time

3. **Adjust the Crop**
   - Grab any corner handle (blue circle)
   - Drag to resize the visible area
   - Watch the percentage update

4. **Fine-tune the Scale**
   - Hold Shift key
   - Scroll mouse wheel up/down
   - Image zooms in/out

5. **Done!**
   - Click "✓ Interactive Mode Active" to disable
   - Settings saved for all posters
